Making a Sandwich

  • 1.Place plate, bread, jelly and butter knife on the counter.
  • 2.Untwist tie on bread package and remove two slices of bread.
  • 3.Place bread onto plate.
  • 4.Retwist tie on bread package and place package on counter.
  • 5.Untwist lid on jelly jar and place lid on counter.
  • 6.Pick up butter knife and insert into jelly to remove some jelly from the jar.
  • 7.Pick up one slice of bread and smooth jelly from the butter knife onto the bread.
  • 8.After filling bread slice with jelly, place butter knife on counter.
  • 9.Pick up bread slice without jelly, place on top of the bread slice with jelly and then put sandwich onto the plate.
